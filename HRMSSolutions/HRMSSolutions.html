HRMS Solutions at Zonixtec

Empower People. Simplify Processes.

At Zonixtec, we build intelligent HRMS solutions that make workforce management effortless. From onboarding and payroll to performance tracking and attendance, our HRMS automates manual HR tasks, reduces paperwork, and provides real-time visibility into employee data—so your HR team can focus on people, not processes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What businesses need an HRMS?
Any organization—startups, SMEs, or enterprises—with growing teams can benefit from an automated HR system.
Can HRMS integrate with biometric systems or attendance devices?
Yes, we offer seamless integration with biometric, RFID, and face-recognition systems.
Is the HRMS cloud-based or on-premise?
We provide both options—depending on your data security and accessibility needs.
Can HRMS handle payroll compliance and taxation?
Absolutely. Our HRMS automates salary slips, deductions, and tax filings with local compliance.
Do you offer customization for HR policies?
Yes, every HRMS module can be customized according to your company's HR structure and approval flow.
