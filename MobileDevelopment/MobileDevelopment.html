Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The timeline varies based on complexity, features, and platform. A basic app can take 2-4 months, while more complex applications may require 6+ months. We provide detailed timelines after understanding your specific requirements.

Should I choose native or cross-platform development?

Native development offers optimal performance and platform-specific features, while cross-platform solutions are more cost-effective and faster to develop. We'll help you choose the right approach based on your budget, timeline, and performance requirements.

How much does mobile app development cost?

Costs vary significantly based on features, complexity, and platforms. Basic apps start around $15,000, while enterprise solutions can range from $50,000 to $200,000+. We provide transparent pricing after a detailed requirements analysis.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, updates for new OS versions, feature enhancements, and performance monitoring. We provide flexible support packages to meet your ongoing needs.

How do you ensure app security?

We implement multiple security layers including data encryption, secure API communication, biometric authentication, code obfuscation, and regular security audits. We follow industry best practices and compliance standards for data protection.
