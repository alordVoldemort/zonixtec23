OUR HISTORY

Zonixtec was founded in 2024 with a simple goal: to build digital solutions that help businesses work smarter. What began with five passionate developers in a small room has now grown into a fast-moving technology company offering CRM, ERP, HRMS, custom software, mobile apps, and cloud solutions.

Our Journey

Your Trusted Partner for Reliable & Scalable Digital Solutions

2024

Foundation

We started Zonixtec with a clear belief that technology should solve real problems, not create new ones. Our first team of five set the tone for innovation and customer-focused development.

2024

First Major Client

Our first custom CRM system delivered a 45% sales improvement for a client. This milestone proved our ability to build impactful, reliable, and scalable business solutions.

2024

Opening Our First Office

We moved from a small room to our first office, creating a dedicated space for collaboration, brainstorming, and stronger teamwork.

2025

New Dedicated Workspace

We set up our full-scale office and expanded our services. With improved infrastructure and a growing team, we strengthened our capabilities in CRM, ERP, custom software, mobile, and web development.

Our Story in a Few Words

Zonixtec has grown from a small team of passionate developers into a trusted technology partner for businesses seeking reliable, modern, and scalable digital solutions. Our journey reflects our commitment to quality, transparency, and continuous improvement.

From building our first CRM solution to expanding into ERP systems, custom software, mobile apps, and cloud services, every milestone has strengthened our mission—to create technology that simplifies operations and drives real business impact.

Today, we continue to grow, innovate, and serve clients across industries with the same dedication we started with. For us, this is just the beginning of a much bigger journey.

Founder's Note

"Some journeys start with code. Ours started with belief."

Zonixtec began with the dream of building technology that helps businesses grow. Every achievement—our first client, our first office, our expanding team—comes from our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer success.
