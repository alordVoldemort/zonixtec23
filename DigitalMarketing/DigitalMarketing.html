Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Marketing, and why is it important?

Digital Marketing uses online strategies—SEO, social media, ads, and content—to grow brand awareness, attract leads, and increase sales.

How long does it take to see results?

Organic strategies like SEO take 3–6 months, while PPC and social campaigns deliver faster, measurable results.

Do you handle both B2B and B2C marketing?

Yes! We tailor strategies for both business types—ensuring the right approach for your target audience.

Can you manage our social media accounts?

Absolutely. Our social media team handles everything from content creation to community management.

How do you measure campaign success?

We track KPIs such as traffic, conversion rates, engagement, and ROI through analytics dashboards and monthly reports.
